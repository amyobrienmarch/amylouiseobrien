The concept behind my project started because of my thesis topic, which I found very interesting and thought it would look good as a piece of design work. Issue 01 Effects of Fast Fashion focuses on the fashion industry and the negative affect its having on our society and landfill. I started researching designers who used big bold text but still good typography as I wanted to the visual language to look cheap and have big bold facts that stood out in the publication. I used a self-covering cover to show the reader that it is a magazine.

ISTD

Landfill is a series of different magazine issues that talks about different topical global problems that are negatively affecting our environment.
