 We worked closely with the 4th years film school in IADT to design a film titles for their gradshow film. The film I was asigned to was Backwater, a short film about a young man living in the west of Ireland, looking after his sick father, feeling very lonely and isolated from the outside world with no relationship with people his own age. I took the symbolism of something drifting through time and no real direction, with a feminine touch of the material, something he doesnt have.
